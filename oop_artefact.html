    Throughout this module, I have diligently engaged with the Codio exercises, studied the extensive literature, and applied my growing knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP). This dedication is evident in the various artifacts I have created, showcasing my understanding and implementation of key OOP concepts such as inheritance, polymorphism, encapsulation, and abstraction. In addition to mastering these OOP principles, I have also focused on system design and the creation of Unified Modeling Language (UML) diagrams. These tools have been instrumental in structuring my approach to software development. By utilizing UML diagrams such as use case diagrams, class diagrams, activity diagrams, sequence diagrams, and state transition diagrams, I have been able to visually map out and refine the architecture and behavior of my driverless car system. This structured design process has ensured that my implementations are not only theoretically sound but also practically robust and scalable.
