    Literature Review At the end of Unit 7, I submitted a Literature Review. The literature review examines the financial impact of ransomware and malware attacks on the financial sector, highlighting costs such as ransom payments, operational disruptions, and reputational damage. It emphasizes the increasing sophistication of cyber threats due to digitization and the reliance on vulnerable legacy systems. Effective mitigation strategies include encryption, endpoint security, collaboration, and robust backup systems to reduce risks and enhance resilience. The study identifies a key challenge in the lack of transparency from financial institutions, which limits accurate assessments of attack impacts. Overall, it underscores the critical need for proactive cybersecurity measures to safeguard the financial sector. Tutor Feedback
