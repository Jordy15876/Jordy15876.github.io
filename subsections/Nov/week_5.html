Week 5


Reading

This week required for the reading of Lopes, A., Valentim, N., Moraes, B., Zilse, R.& Conte, T. (2018) Applying User-centered Techniques to Analyse and Design a Mobile Application. Journal of Software Engineering Research and Development. Where it pretty much presents a set of practical activities applied by a software team using techniques in the analysis and design phases of a mobile application. The techniques include personas, scenarios, and interaction models. The paper suggests employing personas and scenarios to understand requirements, interaction models to understand behavior between user and system. The software industry has applied appropriate techniques to provide a more positive user experience. Through an inspection in the early phases of the development process, it is possible to identify defects in the interaction models and improve software quality.
