Week 7


Activity

This required for the reading of quite of few things but the overall understanding that I gain from this week was on the various Software Development Cycle. A variety of SDLC models are introduced, each with unique benefits and features, including Agile, Fish, V-Shape, Waterfall, Prototyping, and Incremental. The Agile methodology is commended for its adaptability to changing requirements, continuous user involvement, and speed of response. The Fish approach guarantees exceptional output quality even though it is costly and time-consuming. The V-Shape paradigm contains clearly defined steps for coding, validation, and verification that are followed in a sequential manner. The waterfall model is given as a conventional, linear one. The Prototyping approach calls for the development of prototypes for user assessment and input. Requirements are implemented in modules via the incremental model, enabling ongoing development.
