Reading

This week we were required to read chapter 1-5 of Brooks, P. (1975) The Mythical Man-Month - Essays in Software Engineering. Anniversary ed. Addison-Wesley.This was an interesting read and I found the content to be easily digestable. Upon discussion with others I found myself echoing a sentiment which was that this book had given quite a good idea on how to actually manage a project by partitioning a lot of the project task, much of what was discussed in this book is good for a historical and learned text but not something one should really put in practice now.
